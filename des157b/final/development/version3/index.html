For Us

"For Us" is a safe haven for Hmong people to discuss and share their stories through four selected topics: food, art, war, and ghosts. Hmong culture is slowly dissipating due to Westernization and age, so this was designed to safekeep information and stories about Hmong people and culture for future generations.

To participate, select "Write" or "Gallery". “Write” will take you to select any of the four topics: food, art, war, or ghosts. After selecting, fill in the boxes then submit. “Gallery” holds all of the submissions made where you can look at work created by others. Select the desired topic and scroll through the submittied stories. Please refrain from vulgar language when writing and be mindful of other submitted pieces.

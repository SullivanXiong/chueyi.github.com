Project Brief

Project Summary

I want this project to be a haven for Hmong people to discuss their war stories, ghost stories, and the Hmong-American or Hmong-Laotian experience in general for the purpose of sharing and immortalizing Hmong culture which will be designed in a desktop and mobile layout which will be responsive.

Project Goals

  • Provide insight into the Hmong culture
  • Entertainment for ghost stories
  • Act as a haven for discussing the culture
  • Sharing information
  • Discussing fashion
  • Resource for students

Design Strategies

  • Green atmosphere
  • Pattern based
  • Minimal text for design elements
  • User friendly
  • Made to look like an interactable garden

Interaction Strategies

  • Lots of animation
  • Water-like effects
  • Fading text
  • Ripple effect on click, perhaps

Experience Goals

I want users to feel enlightened and more informed about Hmong culture. If they're already well versed in it then I hope that they gain more insight from reading about stories shared by Hmong people.

Users

This website will be designed for Hmong people but not exclusively for them. It can also be a resource for people who are versed in Asian studies and would like to gain insight and information.
